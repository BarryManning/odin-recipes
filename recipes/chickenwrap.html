Chicken Wrap

Description

A savory combination of shredded chicken, texas toast croutons, lettuce and ranch dressing.

Ingredients

  • shredded chicken
  • lettuce
  • croutons
  • ranch dressing
  • tortilla wrap

Steps

  1. set down an open torilla wrap onto a dish
  2. place lettuce onto torilla
  3. place shredded chicken
  4. place croutons
  5. add some ranch dressing
  6. roll up wrap
